Privacy policy

lifedots collects nothing about you.

The app stores three things on your device — your birth date, your chosen life expectancy, and your visual preferences (theme + accent color). Nothing else. None of this ever leaves your phone.

The app does not request the INTERNET permission. It is technically incapable of contacting any server — including the developer's, Google's, or any analytics service. There is no account, no login, no cloud sync, no ads, no third-party SDK, no crash reporting, no telemetry.

If you uninstall lifedots, all data is removed with it.

Data stored on your device

The following is stored locally using Android's DataStore Preferences and never leaves the device:

  • Your birth date
  • Your chosen life expectancy (in years)
  • Your selected theme (light / dark / system)
  • Your selected accent color (brick / sage / brass)

This data is used solely to render the live wallpaper. It is not shared with the developer or any third party.

Permissions requested

android.permission.BIND_WALLPAPER — the system permission required for the app to register itself as a live wallpaper. This is the only permission the app declares.

The app does not declare INTERNET, ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE, storage, location, camera, microphone, contacts, calendar, SMS, phone, or any sensor permission.

Children

The app is suitable for all ages. It is not directed at children specifically and does not collect data from anyone, including children.
